AMBALIKA INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT & TECHNOLOGY

Welcome to Ambalika Institute of Management and Technology, a premier institution committed to shaping the next generation of leaders, innovators, and problem-solvers in the fields of engineering and management. These qualities make AIMT one of the best engineering colleges in Lucknow. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and experiential learning, we provide a dynamic environment where students gain the skills and knowledge needed to excel in today’s fast-paced, ever-evolving global industries. Our distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong industry collaborations ensure that every student receives a well-rounded education, preparing them for success in their careers and beyond. Join us at AIMT—where innovation meets opportunity and ideas turn into impact.

The Ambalika Group

Ambalika Group of Institutions, one of the best engineering colleges in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, was established with the mission of offering high-quality education in the fields of engineering and management. Despite being rich in natural resources, Uttar Pradesh faces challenges in advancing technical and managerial education. This gap has hindered industrial growth and overall economic development in the region. To address this, Ambalika was founded to bridge the gap by expanding access to quality education and fueling future progress.

The group is managed by a dedicated trustee who oversees its diverse business ventures, including Ambalika Construction Limited, Ambalika Chit Fund Limited, and Ambalika Farms & Resorts Ltd. These companies are actively engaged in infrastructure development, building construction, machinery rental, transportation and logistics, earthwork, mining, and crushing. Ambalika Construction Limited serves as the flagship company, driving the group’s core operations.

ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

Ambalika Institute of Management and Technology (AIMT) was established in 2008 as a private engineering and management college in Mohanlalganj, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India, and is affiliated with Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and the Board of Technical Education (BTE), and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

CLASSROOMS

The teaching–learning pedagogy adopted in the classrooms is IT enabled, and almost all rooms are equipped with LCD projectors, OHPs, and sound systems. The lecture halls can accommodate up to 80 students in a single session, while tutorial rooms are designed for smaller groups of 20–30 students.

COLLEGE LIBRARY

The Central Library of Ambalika Institute is a centrally air conditioned, modern, digitized facility spread over 500 sq. m. It is equipped with RFID-based library automation software and houses an international collection of more than 37,030 books across 2,250 titles, providing students with rich scientific and research resources.

LABORATORIES

Laboratories play a vital role in engineering colleges by providing students with practical exposure to complement theoretical learning. They serve as controlled environments where students can experiment, test theories, and analyze outcomes, fostering a deeper understanding of engineering concepts. Laboratories are equipped with advanced tools and technologies tailored to specific engineering disciplines, such as mechanical, civil, electrical, and computer science engineering, enabling hands-on training in areas like prototype testing, circuit design, material analysis, and software simulations.

These facilities are crucial for skill development, allowing students to gain industry-specific expertise and prepare for real-world applications through experimental studies and project-based learning. They also encourage innovation by enabling students to work on individual or team projects using trial-and-error methods to refine their designs. Furthermore, laboratories help students stay updated with modern technological advancements, ensuring they are familiar with current industry standards. Overall, laboratories are indispensable for nurturing the technical proficiency and confidence required for successful engineering careers.

WORKSHOPS

Workshops significantly enhance practical skills in engineering students by providing them with hands-on experiences that complement theoretical learning. These interactive sessions allow students to apply concepts learned in the classroom to real-world situations, thereby deepening their understanding of engineering principles. By engaging in practical activities, students develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities, which are essential for success in the engineering field. Workshops also foster the development of technical skills, such as using tools and equipment, conducting experiments, and troubleshooting, which are crucial for their future careers. Furthermore, these experiences often involve collaboration with peers and industry professionals, enhancing communication skills and preparing students for teamwork in professional settings. Overall, workshops serve as a vital component of engineering education, equipping students with the necessary skills to navigate the complexities of their future careers.
